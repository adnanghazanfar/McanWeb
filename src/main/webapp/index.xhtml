WELCOME!

The Medical Career Advising Network (MCAN) is a new innovative idea to provide much need medical career advising to applicants from all around the world. The idea is to provide a link between medical students and graduates interested to purse a specific career with those that have more experience in that career. In addition to providing very valuable affordable networking experience for young physicians, our goal is to provide funding for non -profit organizations involved in medical education and training in developing countries. One of the organizations we will be funding is EAMTAR, which has been providing much needed medical career guidance to medical students and graduates from Egypt and the world.
