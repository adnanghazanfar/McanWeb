GET STARTED

To start using our service you have to create a profile. Once you create your profile, you can then give or receive career guidance. Here are steps on how to start.

Create a user profile by clicking on Sign up

Start adding your profile information including the following:

  • Personal information: will not be accessible by other members
  • Academic qualifications: your medical school and other degrees you took
  • Clinical qualifications: your medical training, including residency, fellowship and others
  • The skills you can give advice on: anything related to medical training
  • Where you currently live as well as previous locations, you lived in
